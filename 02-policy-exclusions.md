# Apex Auto Insurance — Policy Exclusions

## What Is NOT Covered by Your Apex Auto Policy

Understanding exclusions helps you avoid surprises at claim time. The following situations are excluded from standard Apex Auto Insurance policies.

---

## Intentional Acts
Any damage or injury caused intentionally by the policyholder or a listed driver is not covered. This includes intentionally ramming another vehicle, staging an accident for insurance fraud, or deliberately damaging your own vehicle.

## Racing and Track Use
Damage occurring while your vehicle is used in any organized racing event, speed contest, or on a racetrack is excluded — even if the event is informal or unofficial. This applies to drag racing, autocross, circuit racing, and off-road racing events.

## Commercial Use Exclusions
Standard personal auto policies do NOT cover:
- Delivery services (pizza delivery, Amazon Flex, courier services)
- Rideshare driving (Uber, Lyft) while the app is active and you are carrying or waiting for a passenger
- Taxi or limousine service
- Using your personal vehicle as a business vehicle regularly

**Note**: Apex offers a Rideshare Endorsement add-on for $8/month that covers the gap between your personal policy and the rideshare company's policy.

## Wear and Tear / Mechanical Breakdown
Auto insurance is not a maintenance plan. The following are excluded:
- Engine failure due to lack of oil or overheating
- Brake wear
- Tire wear or blowouts from age
- Rust and corrosion
- Electrical failures from age

## Unlicensed or Excluded Drivers
If a driver not listed on your policy — or specifically excluded from your policy — causes an accident while driving your vehicle, the claim may be denied.

## Using Vehicle Outside the United States
Coverage is limited to the United States, its territories, and Canada. Driving your vehicle into Mexico or other countries is not covered under a standard Apex policy. Separate Mexico auto insurance must be purchased.

## Nuclear, War, and Government Seizure
Damage caused by war, nuclear events, government confiscation, or civil authority action is excluded.

## Modifications and Custom Equipment
Factory-standard equipment is covered. Aftermarket modifications are excluded unless you have purchased the Custom Equipment Endorsement:
- Custom rims and wheels
- Audio/entertainment systems above $1,000
- Lift kits, performance modifications
- Wraps and custom paint

## Property Inside the Vehicle
Personal belongings stolen from or damaged inside your vehicle (laptops, phones, clothing) are NOT covered by auto insurance. These items are covered under your homeowners or renters insurance policy.

## Diminished Value
Apex standard policies do not cover diminished value claims — the reduced resale value of your vehicle after it has been in an accident and repaired.

---

## Important: Always Report First, Assume Second
If you are unsure whether a situation is covered, always file a claim and let Apex's claims team assess it. Do not assume a situation is excluded without consulting an Apex agent.
