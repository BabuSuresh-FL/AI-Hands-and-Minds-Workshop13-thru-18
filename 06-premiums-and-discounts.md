# Apex Auto Insurance — Premiums and Discounts Guide

## How Apex Calculates Your Premium

Your premium is based on risk factors that predict the likelihood and cost of a future claim.

### Primary Rating Factors

**Driver Factors**
- Age and driving experience (young drivers 16–25 pay more; rates improve after 25)
- Driving record (accidents, violations from the past 3–5 years)
- Years licensed
- Credit-based insurance score (permitted in Ohio)
- Annual mileage driven

**Vehicle Factors**
- Make, model, and year
- Vehicle safety ratings (NHTSA/IIHS scores)
- Theft rates for your specific vehicle
- Repair cost index for your vehicle
- Vehicle age and actual cash value

**Coverage Factors**
- Coverage types selected (liability only vs full coverage)
- Deductible amounts (higher deductible = lower premium)
- Coverage limits selected

**Location Factors**
- Zip code (urban areas with higher traffic and theft = higher premium)
- State regulations
- Local weather patterns and catastrophe risk

## Apex Discounts — Full List

### Safe Driver Discounts
- **Clean Record Discount**: No accidents or violations in 3 years — save 10%
- **Accident Forgiveness**: First at-fault accident forgiven after 5 clean years
- **Defensive Driving Course**: Complete approved course — save 5%
- **Apex DriveSmarter Telematics**: Install app or device, drive safely — save up to 20%

### Vehicle Discounts
- **Anti-Theft Device**: Factory or aftermarket alarm — save 5%
- **Anti-Lock Brakes**: All four wheels — save 3%
- **Backup Camera / Blind Spot Monitoring**: Factory-installed — save 2%
- **New Car**: Vehicle is current or prior model year — save 8%
- **Hybrid/Electric Vehicle**: Save 5% on comprehensive coverage

### Policy Discounts
- **Multi-Vehicle**: 2+ vehicles on same Apex policy — save 12% per vehicle
- **Bundle (Auto + Home)**: Save 15% on auto when bundled with Apex home insurance
- **Pay-in-Full**: Pay annual premium upfront — save 5%
- **Paperless Billing**: Enroll in e-statements — save $24/year
- **AutoPay**: Set up automatic payments — save $12/year
- **Early Renewal**: Renew 7+ days before expiration — save 3%

### Customer Loyalty Discounts
- **Loyalty Discount**: 3+ years with Apex — save 5%; 7+ years — save 10%
- **Claim-Free Discount**: 5 years without a claim — save 8%

### Life Stage Discounts
- **Good Student**: Full-time student with 3.0+ GPA — save 10%
- **Student Away at School**: Child on policy but attending college 100+ miles away without a car — save 25% on their portion
- **Mature Driver**: Drivers 55+ who complete safety course — save 5%
- **Military Discount**: Active duty or veteran — save 10%

## Understanding Your Declarations Page

Your Apex Declarations Page (the "Dec Page") summarizes your entire policy:

- **Named Insured**: The primary policyholder
- **Policy Period**: Your coverage start and end dates
- **Vehicles**: Each listed vehicle with VIN
- **Drivers**: All listed drivers
- **Coverage Summary**: Each coverage type with limits and deductibles
- **Premium Breakdown**: Cost per vehicle per coverage
- **Discounts Applied**: All discounts currently applied to your policy
- **Lienholder**: If you have a loan, your lender is listed here

## When Premiums Increase

Apex is required by Ohio law to provide 30 days notice before increasing your premium at renewal. Common causes:
- At-fault accident added to your record
- Traffic violation (speeding, DUI, reckless driving)
- Adding a teen driver
- Statewide rate increase approved by Ohio Department of Insurance
- Credit score decrease
- Dropping below a discount threshold

If you disagree with a premium increase, you have the right to:
1. Request a policy review with your Apex agent
2. File a complaint with the Ohio Department of Insurance (1-800-686-1526)
3. Shop competing insurers (Apex will provide proof of insurance for comparison)
