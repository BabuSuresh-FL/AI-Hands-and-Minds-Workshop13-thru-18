# Apex Auto Insurance — Coverage Types Guide

## Liability Coverage
Liability coverage is required by law in most states. It pays for injuries and property damage you cause to others in an accident where you are at fault.

- **Bodily Injury Liability**: Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and legal fees for people injured in an accident you caused. Apex offers limits from $25,000/$50,000 up to $500,000/$1,000,000 per accident.
- **Property Damage Liability**: Covers repair or replacement of the other party's vehicle or property. Apex offers limits from $25,000 up to $500,000.
- Liability coverage does NOT cover your own injuries or vehicle damage.

## Collision Coverage
Collision coverage pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident involving another vehicle or object (guardrail, tree, pothole), regardless of fault.

- Deductible options at Apex: $250, $500, $1,000, $2,000
- Pays out actual cash value (ACV) of your vehicle minus your deductible
- Required by most lenders if you have a car loan or lease
- Covers rollovers and single-car accidents

## Comprehensive Coverage
Comprehensive coverage pays for damage to your vehicle caused by events other than collisions.

Covered events include:
- Theft or attempted theft
- Weather events: hail, floods, tornadoes, hurricanes, lightning
- Fire
- Falling objects (trees, debris)
- Animal strikes (hitting a deer counts as comprehensive, not collision)
- Vandalism and civil disturbance

Deductible options at Apex: $100, $250, $500, $1,000

## Personal Injury Protection (PIP)
PIP covers medical expenses for you and your passengers regardless of who caused the accident. Also called "no-fault" insurance.

- Covers: medical bills, lost wages (up to 80%), rehabilitation, funeral expenses
- Required in no-fault states; optional in Ohio
- Apex PIP limits: $10,000 / $25,000 / $50,000 per person

## Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage (UM/UIM)
Protects you when the at-fault driver has no insurance or insufficient coverage.

- **Uninsured Motorist (UM)**: Covers your medical bills and vehicle damage when the at-fault driver has zero insurance
- **Underinsured Motorist (UIM)**: Covers the gap when the at-fault driver's coverage is less than your damages
- Apex strongly recommends UM/UIM — approximately 1 in 8 U.S. drivers is uninsured

## Medical Payments Coverage (MedPay)
Covers medical and funeral expenses for you and passengers after an accident, regardless of fault. Simpler than PIP with no wage-loss component.

- Apex MedPay limits: $1,000 / $5,000 / $10,000
- Supplements your health insurance

## Roadside Assistance
Apex Roadside Assistance covers:
- Towing (up to 50 miles)
- Battery jump-start
- Flat tire change
- Fuel delivery (up to 3 gallons)
- Lockout service
Cost: $4/month added to any Apex policy.

## Rental Reimbursement
If your car is being repaired after a covered claim, Apex pays for a rental car.
- Standard: $30/day up to $900 per claim
- Premium: $50/day up to $1,500 per claim
